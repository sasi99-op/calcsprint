Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle information at CalcSprint.

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

1. Data Collection

CalcSprint is designed to operate primarily as a client-side calculator website. This means that all mathematical computations, inputs, and results are processed directly in your web browser using HTML and JavaScript. We do not store, collect, or transmit any calculator input data to our servers.

2. Cookies and Analytics

We may use basic cookies or local storage to save your user preferences (such as preferred unit systems like metric vs. imperial). We may also use privacy-focused analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve user experience. These tools collect anonymous metadata (such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent) and do not collect personally identifiable information.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. If we include links to third-party tools, calculators, or external references, those sites have their own independent privacy policies, and we hold no responsibility or liability for their content and activities.

4. Advertising

To keep CalcSprint 100% free, we may display context-appropriate advertisements. These advertising networks may use non-personal data (such as cookies) to serve relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated modification date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us through our Contact Page.
