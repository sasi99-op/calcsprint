The Compound Interest Formula

For periodic contributions: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt) + PMT × [((1 + r/n)^(nt) − 1) / (r/n)]

Where P = principal, r = annual rate, n = compounding periods/year, t = years, PMT = monthly payment.
