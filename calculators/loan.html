How is the monthly payment calculated?

The standard loan payment formula is: P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1]

Where P = principal, r = monthly interest rate (annual ÷ 12), and n = number of payments. This is called a fully-amortizing loan — each payment covers interest first, then reduces the principal balance.
